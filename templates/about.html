About Us

We are a team of passionate learners and developers working on innovative AI-based solutions to solve real-world problems. This project — Smart Sorting: Identifying Rotten Fruits and Vegetables Using Deep Learning — was developed to help minimize food waste and promote smarter decision-making in storage, retail, and kitchens.

By training a custom machine learning model, we built a web-based application that can automatically classify fruits and vegetables as healthy or rotten with a single image. It’s fast, easy to use, and accessible from any device.

Our goal with this project was not just to build a functional tool, but also to explore the intersection of computer vision and sustainability. We believe in making AI meaningful and accessible — one pixel at a time.

This project reflects our curiosity, collaboration, and commitment to continuous learning.
